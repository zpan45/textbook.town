On a university campus, students frequently buy course textbooks that are used for a few months, only to be left on a shelf for the remainder of their useful life. The process of selling a textbook requires taking a hit of maybe 50% of the value when the book may have a useful life of 3-4 years. The only alternative is a loosely organized Facebook group which only allows crude posts of selling prices or lists of asks. This group has 26,468 Members at Western alone.

Our project aims to allow users to bid on the textbooks of other users, determining fair market values and maximizing the value received by sellers who often do not know how to price their books. Books are posted with 5 photos which represent the best - worst pages in the book which will allow users to assess the quality of the book. Sellers are rated on a long term basis on the honesty of their quality assessments. The trade will take place in the UCC between 9-5 PM. This project is a web-based system with a platform restricted to Western University.
